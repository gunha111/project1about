Famous Japanese Ramen Places

Japan is home to some of the world's most exquisite ramen shops, each offering unique flavors and rich traditions. Here's a look at some of the most famous ramen places you should visit!

Nakiryu

One of Tokyo's Michelin-starred ramen shops, Nakiryu is famous for its rich and creamy tantanmen. It offers a sophisticated dining experience while serving delicious bowls of ramen.

Ichiran

A global favorite, Ichiran specializes in tonkotsu ramen served in private dining booths, allowing you to enjoy the experience undistracted. Its distinctive flavor is adored by ramen lovers worldwide.

Visit Website

Tsuta

Renowned for its shoyu ramen with truffle oil, Tsuta became the world's first Michelin-starred ramen shop. It offers a refined take on the traditional ramen bowl.

Ramen Jiro

Known for its massive portions, Ramen Jiro attracts those seeking hearty and flavorful bowls. It's a popular spot for those who enjoy big meals and bold flavors.

Afuri

Afuri is celebrated for its yuzu-flavored ramen, offering a refreshing twist on traditional recipes. The light, citrusy broth is a fan favorite among ramen enthusiasts.

Ippudo

One of Japan's most popular chains, Ippudo serves creamy tonkotsu ramen with a modern flair. It's known for its stylish interiors and consistent quality.
